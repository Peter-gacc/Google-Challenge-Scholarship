Cheetah

Cheetah is the fastest land animal in the world known to human kind and is mostly found in Africa

  • Speed: 64 mph
  • Habitat:Sub-Saharan Africa
  • Color: tan with black spots
  • Adult weight: 77 to 143 lbs

Cheetah is the fastset animal known to live on the land. It belongs to the cat family but amazingly, it is vey fast, unlike the pets we have. It feeds mostly on small antelope and young of larger animals-Warthog, Kudu, Hartebeest, Oryx. Cheetah are among the indangered species and we need to act fast to save them from extinction
